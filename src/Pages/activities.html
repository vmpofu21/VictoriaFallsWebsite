Must-Do Activities

Devil's pool:

Experience a distinctive view of the falls from Livingstone Island and take a refreshing swim in the Devil's Pool, situated just above the cascade.

Helicopter Ride:

This helicopter tour takes you over the mighty Falls, the Batoka Gorge, the Zambezi River, and the wildlife-filled Zambezi National Park.

Sunset Cruise:

Watch the sun set over the Zambezi River during a cruise, and savor cocktails while observing flocks of birds gracefully soaring in the sky.

White Water Rafting:

Embark on a guided white-water rafting trip adventure that lets you connect more with the Zambezi River than any other touring option.

Couples' Activities

Sunset Dinner Cruise:

Experience the beauty of the sunset and the excitement of one of Africa's most stunning destinations on this Zambezi River dinner cruise.

Victoria Falls Day Trip:

Experience the majestic Victoria Falls from the Zimbabwean and Zambian sides, and visit a local market for souvenirs.

Lookout Cafe:

Perched along the Batoka Gorge, The Lookout Cafe offers unparalleled views, making it one of Victoria Falls' most extraordinary venues.

Boma Dinner and Drum Show:

The Boma Dinner provides guests with an authentic taste of Africa and an opportunity to learn about the local culture.

Wildlife Encounters

Chobe Safari Trip:

Embark on a full-day adventure from Victoria Falls to Chobe National Park, exploring the vibrant wildlife in Botswana's renowned reserve.

Hwange Safari Trip:

Experience the abundant wildlife of Zimbabwe's largest reserve, Hwange National Park, on a full-day excursion from Victoria Falls.

Zambezi National Park:

Explore the diverse wildlife of Zambezi National Park on a full-day journey from Victoria Falls, discovering the beauty of Zimbabwe's nature.

Crocodile Park:

To get a look at the Nile crocodile of the Zambezi River, take a guided tour of Spencer's Creek Crocodile Farm in Victoria Falls.

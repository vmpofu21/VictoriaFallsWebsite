The Lookout Cafe - Wild Horizon

4.6 (1.6k) Restaurant
The Lookout Cafe at Victoria Falls is a breathtaking dining establishment that combines spectacular views with a culinary experience. Perched on the edge of the iconic Batoka Gorge, the cafe offers a panoramic vista of the Zambezi River and the Victoria Falls Bridge. Visitors can indulge in a unique dining experience as they enjoy their meal while taking in the awe-inspiring scenery.

Address: Batoka Gorge, Victoria Falls, Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe
Phone: +263 71 404 2865

The Boma - Dinner and Drum Show

4.4 (1.2k) Dinner Theatre
The restaurant offers an extensive menu featuring traditional dishes that showcase the rich flavors and culinary heritage of Zimbabwe. What sets The Boma apart is its captivating drumshow performance, an exhilarating spectacle that adds rhythm and energy to the dining experience.

Address: Stand 471b Squire Cummings Ave, Victoria Falls, Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe
Phone: +263 83 2843206

The Cassia Restaurant

4.6 (70) Restaurant
The Cassia Restaurant in Victoria Falls offers an unmatched dining experience, providing guests with a delectable à la carte menu available for breakfast, lunch, and dinner. Situated in an idyllic location close to the renowned Victoria Falls, the restaurant has become a favored destination for visitors seeking both culinary delights and scenic beauty.

Address: 411 Livingstone Way, Victoria Falls 00000, Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe
Phone: +263 83 2844737

Jungle Junction Restaurant

4.5 (91) Restaurant
Indulging in a meal at Jungle Junction promises an enduring memory, where panoramic views of the gorge and the mist from the Falls provide a breathtaking backdrop to a fusion of African flavors. Nightly entertainment, featuring diverse ethnic performances, including the enigmatic masked Makishi, captures the essence of Africa.

Address: 1 Mallet Dr, Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe
Phone: +260 97 7711269

MaKuwa-Kuwa Restaurant

4.5 (170) Restaurant
Experience a culinary journey with our à la carte dinner menu, seamlessly blending local produce with global trends, offering delights like coconut-crusted bream fillet and slow-cooked coffee-infused beef short ribs. At lunch, savor favorites such as pulled pork taco and the signature lodge burger in a sweet potato bun, all while enjoying unrivaled views of a wildlife-rich waterhole.

Address: 471d Squire Cummings Rd, Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe
Phone: +263 83 2843206
